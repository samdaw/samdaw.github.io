Everyday distractions and the role of the modern digital user

Attention Distraction is a visual essay that explores the role of the digital user in modern society. The publication was produced to promote BA(Hons) Graphic Design at UWE Bristol and was awarded an ISTD (International Society of Typographic Designers) Certificate of Excellence as it showed a high standard of design and typography. I had a key role in creating the publication and launch event at the Arnolfini.

Services: Graphic Design, Digital Design, Event Planning

Team: Camille Lapham-Flores, Ellen Bills, Chloé Johnston, Rémi Mortimer, Max Malone, Will Richardson and Sam Daw
